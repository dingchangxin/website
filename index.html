About Me

Currently, I am a postdoc at Georgia Tech, supervised by Matt Baker. Before this, I completed my Ph.D. at Brandeis University under the supervision of Olivier Bernardi.

I am interested in the interplay of combinatorics, geometry, and algebra. My recent work focuses on

  • graph bijections related to spanning trees, Jacobian groups, orientations, etc.,
  • their generalizations to ribbon graphs,
  • and matroids and orthogonal matroid (a.k.a. even Δ-matroids) over tracts.
